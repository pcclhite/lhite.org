Welcome

LHITE is an organization at Pasadena City College that advocates the pursuit of computer science.

LHITE, pronounced "light", hopes to elevate the members of PCC's Computer Science community by hosting regular workshops on rising industry trends such as Machine Learning, AI, VR and by exploring very conventional, but powerful skills in Linux, CyberSecurity, and Python.

In the past, LHITE has hosted a wide variety of educational workshops addressing such areas of concentration and serves as a dutiful but meaningful platform for anyone to come and develop their understanding of the vast repository of information in Computer Science.

We welcome any curious minds to participate in our program to see for themselves.
